Who am I?


I was born in Madrid (Spain) in 1989, I have always been passionate about technology, but it wasn't until I needed it that I started to investigate what it was all about. After a long period working in front of the public, I decided to take a break and moved to Brighton (UK), where I perfected some personal skills that I now consider fundamental: languages, communication, self-sufficiency...
I then moved back to Spain, where I started working in a financial services company for 7 years, where, after completing a master's degree in financial management and business control, I was promoted several times until I became Operations Manager of the Spanish subsidiary, in charge of a large commercial team and with huge responsibilities. It was here that I understood the great power of data and the need for good analysis.
At the beginning of the pandemic, and with a lot of free time, I decided to study a master's degree in Data Science, where I discovered that everything I was looking for existed... and much more! After finishing my training, I stayed on campus where I studied, working as a Teacher Assistant in the Data Science module. Now I am working as a Data Analyst in the most important airline in Spain, where I am starting to apply some of the knowledge I have gained, but this is just the beginning.
My medium-term goal is to master data analysis tools to perfection, and together with my experience as an operations manager, complete an interesting profile that can help companies in a technical way, as well as from a business point of view.

If you need more information, feel free to contact me via email, or if you prefer you can check my LinkedIn or my Github.
